🌱 How to use Vaaani

A discovery tutor for the sounds, word-families and meanings of English — at your grade, in your words.

  1. Just ask, then press ↵. Try: "What little piece hides inside triangle, tricycle and tripod?" or "Help me discover what 'aqua' means."
  2. Turn on Guided 🧭. Vaaani never hands you the answer — it leads you to discover it with little questions.
  3. Feel the sounds. Open the Sound Lab (mouth shapes) and the IPA Chart (the real symbols).
  4. Grow word families. Explore Word Roots and trace them across languages on the Language Map.
  5. Check your thinking. Take the Cognitive X-Ray and practice under pressure in the Exam Simulator.

Sound → spelling → word-family → meaning. One discovery at a time. 🌍
