## Migration decisions (make these BEFORE restarting prod)

1. **Legacy documents.** Files ingested before scoping have no owner and become invisible.
   - Single-school/shared deployment: set `VAAANI_LEGACY_DOCS_SHARED=1` (old behaviour for old
     docs; new uploads are still scoped).
   - Multi-tenant (brain.vaaani.in): leave unset and assign owners explicitly:
     `python scripts/assign_owner.py --list` then `--match <name> --user-id <id>`.
   - Emergency escape hatch: `VAAANI_SCOPE_DISABLED=1` restores the old fully-shared behaviour.
2. **memory.json**: legacy facts follow the same policy (hidden unless LEGACY_DOCS_SHARED).
