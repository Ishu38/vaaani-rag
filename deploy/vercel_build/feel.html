Vaaani · Feel the Sound

Feel it, then say it 🤚📳🎤

Hold your phone and tap each word. Voiced sounds buzz — that is your voice-box vibrating; voiceless sounds stay still. Then take your turn: say the buzzing word, and the phone listens to check you voiced it. Feel → say → the phone measures. All on your phone; nothing leaves it.
Tip: put your fingers on your throat and say “zzz” then “sss” — you feel the buzz start and stop. Your phone does the same.
