Frequently asked

Do you store my uploaded documents?

Documents and the knowledge graph live on your own machine when you self-host, and inside an encrypted tenant when you use the hosted version. They are never used to train a third-party model.

Is the Student plan really worth ₹299?

Honest answer: only if you actually use Socratic mode and rate your responses, because that's what powers the skill model. If you only want a chat that summarises PDFs, stay on Free — you get the same retrieval.

What's the refund policy?

Cancel within seven days of your first paid month and we'll refund in full, no questions asked. After that, you can cancel anytime and you keep access until the end of the billing period.

Can my coaching institute pilot Vaaani?

Yes. Two-day in-cohort observation pilots are available — write to us through the contact page.
